NRNumber of spoke holes in the Rm.
NFNumber of spoke holes in the Flange. There are two flanges.
PCD"Pitch Circle Diameter" - diameter of circle running thru the spoke holes in the flange. One for each flange
FtC"Flange to Center" distance. Used in final spoke length calculation only - does't effect display. One for each flange.
OSB"Offset Spoke "Bed". If unknown, this enter 0. It generally has little effect of final spoke length.

The spoke holes in a rim with an OSB will be "offset" to one side, usualy by only 2 to 5 mm. Enter a negative number for the side that the holes are closer to (spokes will be a tiny bit shorter); and enter a positive number for the side that the holes are further from (spoke will be a tiny bit longer).

X"Spoke Cross" value. Number of spokes each spoke crosses on the same side of wheel. One for each flange.
SHD"Spoke Hole Diameter". Like FtC, this value only affects final spoke length, and not as displayed.
